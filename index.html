Top three activities to do in MUMBAI

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus

CSMT, a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Mumbai, is a stunning example of Victorian Gothic architecture with Indian influences, built in 1887. It's a major historical landmark and a bustling railway hub, showcasing grand design and intricate details.

Marine Lines

Marine Lines, located in South Mumbai, is primarily known as the area where the famous Marine Drive promenade begins. It's a significant locality with a major railway station connecting it to the rest of the city.

Gateway of INDIA

The Gateway of India, an iconic arch monument overlooking the Arabian Sea in Mumbai, was built during the British Raj and stands as a symbol of the city's history and a popular tourist hub. From here, ferries depart for Elephanta Caves, and the waterfront buzzes with activity.
